Kristin Esterley - Biographical Information

Kristin Esterley

Kristin Esterley was born in East Lansing, Michigan, but grew up in Davidson, NC.

Kristin earned a B.S. in Mathematics and Religion from Duke Univerisy and a M.S. in Mathematics from UNC Chapel Hill.

After completing her degrees, she worked as a software developer for Andersen Consulting. Clients included the Department of Corrrections for the State of Tennessee, South Carolina Electric & Gas, Glaxo and Carolina Power and Light. After leaving Andersen Consulting, Kristin worked for Glaxo/Glaxo Wellcome.

Kristin left Glaxo Wellcome to raise her two children, Harrison and Katharine. For several years during that time she worked as an Adjunct Instructor of Mathematics and Statistics at Durham Technical Community College.

Kristin has many hobbies that include reading, gardening, hiking and writing. She has two Boston Terriers, Daisy and Henry, and she keeps backyard chickens.
